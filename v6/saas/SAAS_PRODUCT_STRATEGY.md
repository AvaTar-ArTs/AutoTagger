# AutoTag SaaS Product Strategy

## Product thesis

AutoTag SaaS turns chaotic automation/tool directories into actionable intelligence with recurring monitoring and prioritization.

## Users

- Agency owners managing many client tool stacks
- Solo operators with large automation libraries
- Internal ops teams that need inventory + value scoring

## Value pillars

1. **Inventory visibility** - know what exists and where
2. **Intelligence scoring** - business value and integration potential
3. **Execution clarity** - reports that drive next actions
4. **Continuity** - trends over time, not one-off scans

## Feature model

- Scan jobs (manual + scheduled)
- Artifact outputs (JSON, CSV, summaries)
- Comparison snapshots (delta between runs)
- Dashboard views (top assets, stale assets, high-value opportunities)
- Team sharing (links, exports, optional white-label)

## Monetization

- Subscription + seat model
- Add-on services: onboarding, custom taxonomy, enterprise integrations

## Go-to-market sequence

1. Keep V5 as local/open baseline
2. Launch V6 hosted MVP for paid teams
3. Use local->cloud upgrade path as conversion funnel
