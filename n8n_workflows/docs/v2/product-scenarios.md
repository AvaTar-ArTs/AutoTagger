# Trend Pulse — product scenarios (examples)

Short narratives and sample inputs for each workflow. Pair with [`workflow-library-v2.md`](./workflow-library-v2.md) and package READMEs under `n8n_workflows/workflows/*/README.md`.

## trend-analyzer-free

**Trend Analyzer Free** (Free)

### Scenario

You plug in four variants of the same head term, compare intent labels, and export the strongest line for your hero + one bullet for social.

### Sample inputs (illustrative)

- keyword, variant_1, variant_2, intent

### Expected shape of outputs

- Single-keyword analysis plus CTA-friendly summary for landing pages.
