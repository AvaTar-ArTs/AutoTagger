Database Structure

Main Noi Databases

  • History DB: 192KB - Tracks all browsing and conversation history
  • Ask Log DB: 48KB - Stores AI conversation logs and prompts
  • Prompts DB: 76KB - Manages custom prompts and templates

Service-Specific Storage

Each AI service maintains separate IndexedDB storage:

  • Located at: ~/Library/Application Support/Noi/Partitions/noi_main/IndexedDB/
  • Separate LevelDB files for each service (e.g., https_z.ai_0.indexeddb.leveldb)
  • Preserves login state, preferences, and conversation history independently
